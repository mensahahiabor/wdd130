Ghana

Ghana, located on the Gulf of Guinea in West Africa, is a vibrant country known for its rich history, diverse cultures, and warm hospitality. Often called the "Land of Gold" for its historic mineral wealth, it was also a key hub in the transatlantic slave trade, leaving behind poignant landmarks like Cape Coast Castle and Elmina Castle. Cultural Heritage: Home to over 100 ethnic groups, including the Ashanti, Fante, and Ewe, with colorful festivals like Akwasidae and Hogbetsotso. Wildlife & Nature: From the lush Kakum National Park (with its canopy walkway) to the scenic Wli Waterfalls and golden beaches of Busua. Modern Vibes: Accra, the capital, blends contemporary art, buzzing nightlife, and historic sites like Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum. Foodie Paradise: Try jollof rice, banku with tilapia, kelewele (spicy fried plantains), and fresh coconut juice.
