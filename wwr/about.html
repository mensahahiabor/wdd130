Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best rafting experience with safety, adventure, and fun at the core of our values. We believe that every person deserves to experience the raw power and beauty of nature while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship. Our expert guides are passionate about sharing their knowledge and ensuring that every adventure creates lasting memories.

Our History

Founded in 1995, Splash White Water Rafting has been offering top-notch rafting adventures for over 25 years. We pride ourselves on creating unforgettable experiences for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Starting with just two rafts and a dream, we've grown to become the premier white water rafting company in the region, serving thousands of adventurers annually while maintaining our commitment to safety, sustainability, and exceptional customer service.
